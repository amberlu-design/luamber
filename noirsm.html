Noirdes & Co Marketing





Overview

Conduct market research to determine branding and social media marketing strategy.

Scope: Brand Position, Brand Strategy, Social Media Marketing, Content Creation

Duration: 6 Months



Brand Position + Strategy

As a startup, we focused on branding and positioning before proceeding to other elements of business development. We took into consideration the brand’s designed pieces, target audience, price point, and positioning statements.

Noirdes & Co’s slogan is “For the bold and sophisticated.” The founders started the company because they wanted to provide quality and timeless jewelry pieces at a price point relatively cheaper than luxury brands like Tiffany & Co and Chanel but also higher than common brands like Forever 21 to establish the quality of their pieces. With that in mind, I put together different mood boards, color palettes and simple adjectives to see which brand persona most closely align with the vision of the brand.

Bold. Sweetheart. Ambitious.

Fun. Adventurous. Brave.

Classy. Elegant. Soft.


We eventually decided to go for option 1 because it’s the most accurate depiction of Noirdes & Co.


Content Creation

Since the mission of Noirdes & Co is to deliver high quality and timeless jewelry, I created marketing content that shows both the close up of the design details and just simple and everyday-life-like content. By including a lot of skin color and hand-shots in action, we successfully deliver the vision of the brand.

To deliver a sense of sophistication with a nude color palette, I tried to limit the editing and filters applied to the photos and to create something that doesn’t require much editing, I used fabrics that reflects light off of the pieces as backgrounds to make the pieces stand out. I also incorporated a lot of stones to contrast the texture and color of the hand and the pieces.



Social Media Marketing

Having a visually appealing Instagram feed that tells a story is extremely important for social media marketing. This is the first thing that the audiences see and within a few seconds, they will decide whether this is a brand for them to keep shopping at or not.

It is challenging to grow as a newly started business on Instagram because there are so many competing brands on there that have already established their core audiences and a distinct online persona. A competitive advantage Noirdes & Co has is its unique brand position in the market, so it’s important to deliver that through the curated content.

Planoly is used to plan and schedule posts beforehand to make sure our content go live in time and maintain the brand persona.

Instagram insights were used to track data and analytics that give us a better insight into how we are doing and where we could focus more on.

On top of the content we created, we also used contents created by the influencers that we partner with and filler photos to promote the feeling of not only the products but a lifestyle as well.
